Thoothukudi Biriyani Recipe

Ingredients

For Masala

  • cinnamon
  • cardamom
  • cloves
  • saunf
  • whole pepper
  • biriyani leaves

For Sauteing

  • oil
  • cinnamon
  • cardamom
  • cloves
  • biriyani leaves
  • nutmeg
  • star anise
  • dried sea weed
  • onion
  • tomato
  • ginger + garlic paste

Other

  • rice
  • water
  • chilli powder
  • garam masala
  • salt

Cooking Steps

  1. Add oil to a pot and let it heat up (Don't let it burn / smoke).
  2. Once it is heated up, add the ingredients for sauteing (Don't let it burn)
  3. Once all the ingredients are sauteed well and the oil is separated, add the masala
  4. Once the masala is well integrated, add water necessary for cooking rice and let it boil
  5. Once the water boils, add rice and simmer the flame. Allow the rice to cook well.
  6. Add salt to taste once rice is added, add chilli powder for heat and additional garam masala if the biriyani masala is not enough
  7. Check it every so often and mix it if necessary.
